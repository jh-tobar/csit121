Barbie (2023)

Directed by Greta Gerwig

I would say Barbie by Greta Gerwig was the best movie of 2023. It brought me back to my childhood, from the cool sets and wardrobes, to the canceled-dolls references. A perfect 10/10 movie.

The movie had notable cast members like:

  • Margot Robbie
  • Ryan Gosling
  • Will Ferrell
  • America Ferrera
  • Kate McKinnon
  • Micheal Cera

The best scenes would be:

  1. When Barbie started shifting to the real world.
  2. When all the Barbies tricked all the Kens.
  3. The "Im Just Ken" scene.
